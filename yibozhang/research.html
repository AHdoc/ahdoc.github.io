I am interested in low-dimensional topology, hyperbolic geometry, their moduli and dynamics. In particular, I focus on 3-manifolds and 4-manifolds, singular fibrations and Teichmuller theory. I am also interested in complex hyperbolic geometry, symplectic geometry and geometric group theory.
