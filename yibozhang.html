The thesis defense took place on April 5th, 2024 in Grenoble. (thesis; slides)

I am a third-year PhD student at Institut Fourier under the supervision of Louis Funar and Greg McShane. Before coming to Grenoble, I studied at Université du Luxembourg. Before coming to Luxembourg, I worked at Accutar Biotech. Before that, I studied at Fudan University.

A short CV is here.

I am interested in low-dimensional topology, hyperbolic geometry, their moduli and dynamics. In particular, I focus on 3-manifolds and 4-manifolds, singular fibrations and Teichmuller theory. I am also interested in complex hyperbolic geometry, symplectic geometry and geometric group theory.
